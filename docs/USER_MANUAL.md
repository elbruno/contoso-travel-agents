## Planning Your Trip

### Step-by-Step Trip Planning

#### Step 1: Choose Your Destination

1. Browse the **Featured Travel Deals** carousel
2. Click through different destinations
3. Note the discount percentages
4. Consider what interests you:
   - Nature & Adventure (Iceland, Switzerland)
   - Culture & History (Morocco, Italy)
   - Modern & Traditional (Japan)

#### Step 2: Open the AI Chat

1. Click the floating **chat button** (bottom-right)
2. Review the sample prompts
3. Choose a starting point

#### Step 3: Provide Your Details

Share information like:
- **Destination**: Where you want to go
- **Duration**: How many days
- **Budget**: Approximate spending limit
- **Dates**: When you want to travel (or flexibility)
- **Travel Style**: Adventure, relaxation, culture, food
- **Group**: Solo, couple, family, friends

**Example Message:**
```
I'm planning a 10-day trip to Iceland from Paris. 
Budget is around 5000 euros. 
I love adventure and want to see waterfalls and glaciers.
Best time to visit?
```

#### Step 4: Review AI Recommendations

The AI will provide:
- **Itinerary Suggestions**: Day-by-day plans
- **Must-See Attractions**: Top destinations
- **Local Experiences**: Authentic activities
- **Travel Tips**: Practical advice
- **Budget Breakdown**: Cost estimates
- **Best Times**: Seasonal recommendations

#### Step 5: Ask Follow-Up Questions

Refine your plan by asking:
- "Can you make it more budget-friendly?"
- "What about accommodations?"
- "How do I get around?"
- "What should I pack?"
- "Any local food recommendations?"

#### Step 6: Save Your Itinerary

- **Copy text**: Select and copy chat responses
- **Screenshot**: Take screenshot of important details
- **Email yourself**: Copy and email the conversation
- **Bookmark page**: Return later to continue chat

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Chat Not Responding

**Problem**: AI doesn't reply to messages

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily

#### Images Not Loading

**Problem**: Carousel images don't display

**Solutions**:
1. Check internet speed
2. Disable ad blockers
3. Allow images in browser settings
4. Try incognito/private mode
5. Refresh the page

#### Chat Button Not Visible

**Problem**: Can't find the floating chat button

**Solutions**:
1. Scroll to bottom-right of page
2. Check if it's hidden behind other windows
3. Try different browser
4. Zoom out if page is zoomed in
5. Refresh the page

#### Carousel Not Moving

**Problem**: Can't navigate carousel

**Solutions**:
1. Click directly on arrow buttons
2. Try dot indicators instead
3. Disable browser JavaScript blockers
4. Refresh the page
5. Try different browser

#### Mobile Display Issues

**Problem**: Layout looks broken on mobile

**Solutions**:
1. Rotate device (portrait/landscape)
2. Close other browser tabs
3. Update browser to latest version
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try default browser

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

**Q: Is Zava Travel Agents free to use?**
A: Yes, browsing deals and using the AI chat assistant is completely free.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Currently, no account is required to use the chat and browse deals.

**Q: Can I book trips directly through the website?**
A: The AI provides recommendations and planning. Booking links may be provided for partner services.

**Q: Is my chat history saved?**
A: Chat history is saved in your browser session but not permanently stored on servers.

### AI Chat Questions

**Q: How does the AI assistant work?**
A: It uses Azure AI Foundry agents powered by advanced language models to provide travel recommendations.

**Q: Is the AI information accurate?**
A: The AI provides helpful suggestions but always verify critical information like visa requirements and prices.

**Q: Can the AI make reservations for me?**
A: No, the AI is for planning and recommendations only. Use provided links for actual bookings.

**Q: What languages does the AI support?**
A: Currently English, with more languages coming soon.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is the page loading slowly?**
A: Check your internet connection. The site uses optimized assets for fast loading.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! The website is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Does the chat work offline?**
A: No, an internet connection is required for the AI chat.

**Q: How do I clear my chat history?**
A: Click the "New Chat" button (refresh icon) in the chat header.
